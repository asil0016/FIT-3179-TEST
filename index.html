Total Energy Consumption in Australia

In this visualisation, there will be 5 visualisation which relate to the total energy consumption in Australia

Total Energy Consumption by Type of Industry

Below is the visualisation that shows the total energy consumption based on type of industry. The total energy consumption is in PJ(Peta Joule). Transport Industry is the industry that consume energy the most in every year from 2010 until 2020 while, the lowest is Water and waste Industry

Total Energy Consumption by Type of Fuel

This visualisation show a pie chart which present the percentage of energy consumption by type of fuel. There are 5 type of fuel, oil, gas, electricity, renewables, and coal. Oil has the highest percentage in each year and constanly at the lowest is coal.

Total Energy Consumption per State

For total energy consumption per state, map chart is used to show the value of total energy consumption based on the colour saturation. The darker colour shows that the state consumed more energy than other state and vice versa
